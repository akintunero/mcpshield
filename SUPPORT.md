## Before Opening an Issue

1. Check the [troubleshooting guide](docs/troubleshooting.md)
2. Search [existing issues](https://github.com/akintunero/mcpshield/issues)
3. Check your `.env` configuration
4. Ensure LocalStack or your AWS endpoint is running
